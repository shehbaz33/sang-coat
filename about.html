About Sang Coat

Sang Coat boasts a rich and storied history that spans over four remarkable decades. As a family-owned business, our legacy is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence and our relentless pursuit of perfection in the field of anodizing and coating. Our journey began as a humble workshop, where we labored tirelessly, driven by a deep passion for our craft. Over the years, our dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction propelled us forward, paving the way for our ascent to becoming a renowned and respected name in the anodizing and coating industry.

Our Story

Sang Coat has been a family-owned business for over four decades. We take immense pride in our journey from a small workshop to a renowned name in the anodizing and coating industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet significant - to bring charm and durability to your home interiors and exteriors. We accomplish this by consistently delivering high-quality anodizing, electro colours anodizing, powder coating, and polyurethane painting services.

Our Team

Behind our success is a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about what they do. From skilled technicians to experienced painters, each member of our team plays a crucial role in ensuring the satisfaction of our valued clients.
